Account Deletion – Mindbody Go

Mindbody Go, developed by Sniper Vans Studio, allows users to request permanent deletion of their account and all associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Mindbody Go app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap "Delete Account" and confirm your choice.

Once confirmed, your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This includes your profile, bookings, and any stored preferences.

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion via email: support@yourdomain.com
